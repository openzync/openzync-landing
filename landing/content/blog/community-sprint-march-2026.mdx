Our March 2026 community sprint brought together **40+ contributors** from 12 countries. Here's what we accomplished.

## Highlights

- **New FalkorDB backend** — Now in beta, with full query support
- **TypeScript SDK initial release** — For Node.js and browser environments
- **Documentation overhaul** — Restructured around developer journeys
- **25+ bugs squashed** — Including 3 security-related fixes

The community voted on priorities for the next sprint:

1. Kafka integration for event streaming
2. Expanded tutorial series
3. Performance benchmarking suite
