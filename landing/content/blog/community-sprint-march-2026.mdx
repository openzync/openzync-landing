Our March 2026 community sprint brought together contributors from around the world. Here's what we accomplished.

## Highlights

- **New FalkorDB backend** — Now available with full query support and GraphBLAS traversal
- **SurrealDB backend improvements** — Enhanced BM25 full-text search and schema bootstrap
- **Documentation overhaul** — Restructured around developer journeys
- **Bug fixes** — Multiple issues resolved across the codebase

## What's Next

The community voted on priorities for the next sprint:

1. Enhanced MCP server tools
2. Expanded tutorial series
3. Performance benchmarking suite
